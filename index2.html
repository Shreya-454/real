The Real Community is on Workvivo

With thousands of agents across the US and Canada, we needed a virtual community where all Real agents could connect. That’s where Workvivo comes in!

Access Workvivo Easily through reZEN

You’re just one click away from Workvivo on any device – no URLs or passwords needed. Simply open reZEN, tap Workvivo, and you’re in!

Check out some of our favorite features:

Connect with Local Agents

Click the Explore tab at the top, then click Spaces to find your state group and start connecting with agents in your area.

Stay Informed

Catch the latest insights from Tamir, Sharran, and the rest of Real's leadership team by clicking the Explore tab at the top, then clicking to open the News section, where all key Real updates are waiting for you.

Share and Learn with 22K+ Agents

For quick access, star your favorite groups by clicking the star icon in the top left of the group, then find all your starred groups by clicking the Explore tab at the top left, then clicking Spaces and Starred.

For the best experience, don’t forget to download the Workvivo mobile app!

To set it up correctly:

Download the Workvivo app.

Open the reZEN app.

Tap the Workvivo button in reZEN to connect seamlessly.
